About

With the increasing complexity of architectural form and the performance demands on the building, we believe it is essential to visualize and understand its components at an early stage of the project. We have collaborated in assisting and designing innumerous innovative products for renowned architects. Our Studio consists of experts with great passion and knowledge in architecture, design and advanced modeling. At STUDIOR73, we provide:

  • Advanced Visualization

  • Concept Design
  • Means and Methods Plan
  • Parametric Modeling
  • Schedule Coordination
  • System Solutions
